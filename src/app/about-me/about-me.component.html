Who am I?

I'm a web developer located in sunny (sometimes) Florida. I love tackling interesting, difficult projects to expand my experience and expertise in the field. The pace of progress on the web has become extremely rapid, and I'm always looking for new ways to make my code faster, simpler and more robust. I've been coding for three years and have found a passion in crafting accessible, functional applications.

Philosophy

The web has grown into an amazing platform for application development, and I'm fascinated by its potential. It is uniquely suited for ubiquitous delivery of content and allows for the greatest possible reach for the modern internet - global audiences and diverse devices. It provides for an incredibly flexible approach towards building applications that are used by people and businesses all over the world.

Real world applications should be intuitive, clean, fun to use and provide value to everyday users. That's what I strive for in every project I develop. I enjoy learning new technologies and experimenting with the cutting-edge in order to provide for the richest possible web experience.
